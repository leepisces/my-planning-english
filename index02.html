🎯 Effective Study Tips

  • Focus on one group at a time - Master related vocabulary together for better retention
  • Create example sentences - Use words from the same group in context to see their relationships
  • Use spaced repetition - Review each group after 1 day, 3 days, 1 week, and 2 weeks
  • Make connections - Compare and contrast words within each group to understand nuances
  • Apply in conversation - Try to use words from your current study group in real conversations
